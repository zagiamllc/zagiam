Zagiam LLC is a holding company focused on building brands in two direct-to-consumer verticals: subscription mail clubs and print-on-demand products. Each brand in the portfolio is independently developed with its own identity, audience, and product strategy.

We operate as a single corporate structure supporting multiple brands — allowing each one to grow on its own terms while sharing the infrastructure, values, and long-term vision of the company behind it.

Zagiam LLC is a holding company built for modern commerce, creating meaningful brands through on-demand products and curated experiences.

Mission

To transform ideas into scalable brand ecosystems.

We don't chase trends or volume. Zagiam exists to take a clear idea, build it into a brand with real structure, and grow it into something that serves a community over the long term. Every brand under Zagiam is built to scale — not to spike.

Vision

To operate as a modern holding company where creativity and operational intelligence coexist.

The goal is a house of brands where creative vision and business discipline work together — not against each other. Built independently. Grown sustainably. Owned entirely.
